About Gabriel and DMD

Gabriel is a lovely 8 year old boy who goes to the same school as my boys. When he was 3 he was diagnosed with Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD), a progressive muscle wasting disease. The muscles slowly waste away, first those of the hips, pelvic area, thighs and shoulders, and later the arms, legs and trunk. By the early teens, the heart and respiratory muscles are also affected. DMD is fatal, with life expectancy of late teens to mid twenties.

Gabriel's parents Ana and Steve set up Hope for Gabriel to spread awareness about DMD and raise money to fund research into a cure for Duchenne. Speaking to some of the world's organisations fighting to find a cure for Duchenne gave them hope that something might happen in time to save Gabriel and all others living with this disease. Some human clinical trials are under way.
